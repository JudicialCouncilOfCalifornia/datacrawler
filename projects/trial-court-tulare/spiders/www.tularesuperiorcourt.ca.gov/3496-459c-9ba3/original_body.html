divisions


Here you will find information regarding the various divisions at the Superior Court of California, County of Tulare. Select from the options below to learn more about each department.

Appeals


Get information on the appeals process for Felony, Juvenile Delinquency, Juvenile Writ Cases, Limited and Unlimited Civil Cases, Settlement/Abandonment/Voluntary Dismissal/Compromise appeal processes.

Civil


The Court Civil Division handles civil disputes, including landlord/tenant conflicts.

Collections


The Court Collection Department provides enforcement of court-ordered traffic and criminal fines for the Visalia, Tulare, Porterville, and Dinuba Superior Courts.

Criminal


The Superior Court has jurisdiction over misdemeanor and felony criminal charges. Misdemeanors are offenses generally punishable by a fine and/or a county jail term, and felonies are generally punishable by imprisonment in the state prison and/or fines.

Family Court Services


The Family & Child Division hears matters involving family-related issues and domestic relations.

Family Law


Information relating to divorce, legal separation, annulments, custody and visitation, and more.

Juvenile


The Juvenile Divison handles juvenile delinquency and juvenile dependency cases.

Probate


The Probate Department of the court handles decedents' estates, trusts, guardianships of minors, and conservatorships of adults.

Small Claims


Small claims court is a special court where disputes are resolved quickly and inexpensively. The rules are simple and informal.

Traffic


The Traffic Division has jurisdiction over Vehicle Code (infraction) violations, some local municipal ordinances and minor misdemeanors.
