        Mediation Division

        Lake County currently offers mediation services for Child Custody matters. Mediation is a type of dispute resolution that is an alternative to litigation. Mediation is provided free of charge and is an opportunity to resolve issues without a court hearing.

        Lake County offers mediation assistance in the following areas:

        • Family Court Services Child Custody Mediation
        • Civil Mediation information coming soon.

        Child Custody Mediation Parent Orientation Class is held on the 2nd and 4th Tuesdays of the month at 12:00pm in Conference Room C on the Third Floor of the Lakeport Courthouse. Contact the Lake County Superior Court with questions.
