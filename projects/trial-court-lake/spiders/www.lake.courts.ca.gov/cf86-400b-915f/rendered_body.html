        Self Help

        Lake County Superior Court Self-Help Center and Family Law Facilitator's Office

        California Online Self-Help Center | Centro de ayuda en linea

        Services Provided:

        Divorce, Legal separation, Establish paternity, Modify child custody and visitation orders, Modify child and spousal support orders, Civil Harassment restraining orders, Guardianships (person only), Residential evictions, Name changes, Small claims, and actions involving the Department of Child Support Services.

        Assistance Available By:

        Individual appointment, walk-in, workshops, telephone, mail and fax.

        Reference and Educational Materials:

        Checklists for completing and filing court forms, self-help forms, self-help books, computer access to legal forms and programs, local attorney lists, and reference lists for community resources and organizations.

        Self Help Center Workshops:

        Workshop Information

        The Self Help Center will now be offering a workshop on Trial Preparation for Self-Represented Litigants every Thursday from 1-2:30. Please call 707-994-4612 to reserve your spot.

        Start Your Paperwork:

        LawHelp Interactive is a web platform administered by the nonprofit organization, Pro Bono Net, that helps self-represented litigants fill out their paperwork. We strongly recommend making an account and having your paperwork reviewed by the Self-Help Center before filing.
