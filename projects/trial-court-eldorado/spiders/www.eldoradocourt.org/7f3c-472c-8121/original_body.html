Court Reporter Transcripts

You can order a court reporter transcript by email, drop box, or mail. Please submit a completed Transcript Order FormLocal form M-33
to:

Email transcriptorders@eldoradocourt.org
Mail/Drop Box Superior Court of California
ATTN: Transcripts
3321 Cameron Park Drive
Cameron Park, CA 95682

If you have questions or need further assistance, please call (530) 621-6718.



Electronic Audio Recording

Pursuant to Government Code Section 69957, the Court may utilize an electronic audio recording device to generate a verbatim record of court proceedings in applicable instances where the services of an official court reporter cannot be obtained. Electronic recording may be used to capture court proceedings in Department 5 and Department 9. Parties may refer to their minute order to determine if electronic audio recording was used.

Transcript of Electronic Audio Recording

You may request a transcript for electronically recorded court proceedings by contacting the third-party vendor For The Record. To request a transcript of an electronic audio recording, please click the link below. You will be routed to the For The Record transcript ordering platform.

To order a transcript, you must have the case number, hearing date, hearing department and approximate time the hearing took place.

Upon payment, the transcript will be sent to you electronically or in paper format, if requested.
