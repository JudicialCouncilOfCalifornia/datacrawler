Find & Pay Citations
Print

You have two options to pay, either by using your credit card or electronic fund transfer directly from your checking or savings account. There are fees associated with each transaction no matter what option you choose.


You must have your case number and payment amount available to make an electronic payment. If you do not know your case number, you can follow the links below to find it -

  • Traffic Case Index
  • Criminal Case Index

    • NOTE: If your case has been referred to collections you must pay your fines and fees directly to Linebarger Goggan Blair & Sampson, LLP. Click here for collection information.
