Divisions

 
Napa County Superior Court is committed to meeting your needs.  For more information, please select from one of the divisions below.

 

Print | E-mail

Civil

The Civil Division provides legal processing services to individuals and businesses of Napa County in the areas of Family Law, Probate Guardianships/Conservatorships, Mental Health, General Civil both limited and unlimited jurisdictions.

MORE»

Criminal

The Criminal Filing Division is responsible for filing Criminal Complaints submitted by the District Attorney’s Office. In addition court staff provides information about Court dates, appearance requirements, fine information, warrants, and record searches.

MORE »

Family

The Civil Division provides legal processing services to individuals and businesses of Napa County in the areas of Family Law, Probate Guardianships/Conservatorships, Mental Health, General Civil both limited and unlimited jurisdictions.

MORE »

Jury

If you have been summoned for jury service you may use our self service system and your juror badge number located on the bottom of your summons to check and confirm service or postpone service.

MORE »

Juvenile

Only persons with a legal right to do so may receive information regarding juvenile cases, such as a parent or legal guardian of the minor. Juvenile matters require a personal appearance with proper photo identification. Information regarding Juvenile matters is confidential and cannot be given over the phone

MORE »

Traffic

The Traffic Division is responsible for filing citations from various law enforcement agencies in Napa County. Customer Service is provided 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. If you have received a citation in Napa County you can access the courts  automated system to pay a fine, sign-up for traffic school or find helpful information about the citation process anytime it is convenient for you.
