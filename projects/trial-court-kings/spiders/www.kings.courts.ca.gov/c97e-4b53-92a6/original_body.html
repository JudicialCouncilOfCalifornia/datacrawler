        Location & Contact Info

        The Kings Superior Court building is a FULL SECURITY facility. All persons entering the buildings will be required to pass through a weapons screening device and have all hand-carried objects x-rayed. Shorts, tank tops, crop tops and bare feet are not permitted in any of the Kings Superior Court facilities. Read more information about Court Etiquette and Security.
