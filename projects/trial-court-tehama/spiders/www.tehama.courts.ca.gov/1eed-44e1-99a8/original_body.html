    Welcome to Tehama Superior Court’s Online Calendar Portal

    The Tehama Superior Court maintains this website as a public service to enhance access to public information.  This service is continually under development, and will be updated as events take place and court resources permit.

The most current filings may not be reflected in the site’s online features, check back frequently as calendars periodically update.
