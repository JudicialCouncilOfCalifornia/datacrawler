Local Rules

Complete Local Rules of Court

In addition to these local rules, Siskiyou Superior Court enforces all applicable state statutes and the California Rules of Court. Counsel and self-represented litigants are responsible for knowing and complying with applicable state statutes and the California Rules of Court. In general, the local rules do not contain rules that are duplicative of state statutes or the California Rules of Court.

Fax Filing

The Court accepts fax filings for the following matters by facsimile transmission pursuant to California Rules of Court, Rules 2.300 through 2.306:

Civil/Probate: (530) 842-0164
Criminal: (530) 842-8178
Family Law: (530) 842-0191
Appellate: (530) 842-0164

File Documents 24 hours a day, 7 days a week

Documents received by 5 p.m. shall be filed with that day’s date. All documents received after 5 p.m. will be deemed filed on the next business day.

For documents requiring a filing fee refer to Local Rule 2.15
